Code of Conduct
• Disrespect of any kind will not be tolerated and punishment(s) will be given out swiftly.
• Abide by the rules of the TeamSpeak server and its channels as prescribed by the channel description or any command presesnt in thus channel.
• RolePlay actions that aim to offend or incite a negative reaction on the basis of race/ethnicity, religion, gender, sexaul orientation, nationality or political belief will not be tolerated!
• This community has a Zero Tolerance Policy for discrimination of any kind, especially on the basis of race/ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orienation, nationality or political belief. You will be banned without warning if you are caught discriminating against Anyone, in-character or out of character.
• All bans under the duration of a month are NOT ELIGIBLE for consideration for the appeal process.
