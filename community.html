Within the PASRP RolePlay Community we like to explore different ideas and test different things that may or may not end up being good for members. We try are best to have a great Community and explore within the Community.

Community Requirments

    1. To all rules there are reasonable limits and restrictions set by community staff.
    2. While we permit dual clanning, we do not allow dul staffing. If you are a staff member of another FiveM community you will not be eligible for a promotion into a staff position with PASRP
    3. All registered members must have their call sign or civilian rank in their Steam name or FiveM name. If you are playing on our server and you are not a member, you are not permitted to utilize any callsign or civlian rank found on the PASRP Roster.
    4. You must be on the Teamspeak whille connected to the server. If you are active as LEO/Fire You must be in RTO you must be in RTO. If you are active as Civ you must be in a civilian zone channel.
    5. Your Teamspeak name must match your Steam/FiveM/Roster name.
    6. No colored text or symbols(Limited) in Steam/FiveM names.
    7. No more than 2 transfers between departments in a 4 month period will be permitted. Additionally, you may only transfer once a month. NO EXCEPTIONS!
